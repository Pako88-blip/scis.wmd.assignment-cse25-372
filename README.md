Website Name
Kgomo Care Livestock Veterinary Services
Website Objective
The objective of the Kgomo Care website is to provide livestock farmers in Botswana with easy and convenient access to veterinary services, livestock health information, and a catalogue of available livestock vaccines and animal health products.
The website will serve as an online information hub where farmers can:

Website Goals
The goals of the Kgomo Care website are to:
1.	Provide reliable veterinary and livestock health information for farmers.
2.	Promote veterinary services such as farm visits, disease diagnosis, and livestock vaccination.
3.	Display a catalogue of livestock vaccines and animal health products for cattle, goats, sheep, and poultry.
4.	Allow farmers to contact the clinic or request veterinary assistance through an online form.
5.	Improve communication between livestock farmers and veterinary professionals.
6.	Increase awareness of livestock disease prevention and proper animal care.
