About - Kgomo Care

Who We Are

Kgomo Care is a livestock veterinary service dedicated to improving animal health and supporting farmers across Botswana. We provide reliable veterinary services, health education, and access to essential livestock vaccines.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve livestock health management by providing accessible, affordable, and professional veterinary services while educating farmers on disease prevention and proper animal care.

What We Do

Farm Visits

Disease Control

We help prevent and treat livestock diseases.

Farmer Education

We provide guidance on livestock health and management.
